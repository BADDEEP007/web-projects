About Me.

Hello! I’m Rouchi Silawat, a passionate and innovative product designer in my college years, focused on creating designs that are not only visually appealing but also meaningful and functional. With a knack for aesthetics and a deep understanding of design fundamentals, I’ve been crafting everything from product packaging to mockups, social media posts, and vector art. My work spans various domains, including food products, cosmetics, and lifestyle goods.

Using SolidWorks and other design tools, I’m always eager to bring fresh ideas to life, whether it’s through product design, branding, or packaging. I believe that each design tells a story, and my goal is to craft narratives that resonate with people while meeting the highest standards of quality.

This portfolio showcases a selection of my projects, highlighting my skills in transforming concepts into engaging and market-ready designs. I’m excited to share my journey and continue pushing the boundaries of creativity and design innovation!
